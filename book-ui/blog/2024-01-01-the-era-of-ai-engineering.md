## What is AI Engineering?

Traditionally, software engineering involved writing logic line-by-line. **AI Engineering** is different. It focuses on designing systems where AI models (like GPT-4, Claude, or Llama) act as core components of the application logic.

> **Definition:** AI Engineering is the practice of building robust, scalable, and reliable systems that integrate Artificial Intelligence to solve complex problems.

## The Spec-Driven Philosophy

In this article, we explore a unique methodology called **Spec-Driven Development**.

1.  **Specification:** We define *what* we want in plain English (or structured prompts).
2.  **Generation:** We use AI Agents to write the initial code and documentation.
3.  **Verification:** We review the output against our strict specifications.
